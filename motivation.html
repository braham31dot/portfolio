My Motivation

My passion for design is rooted in the process of bringing ideas to life in 2D and 3D, whether it's visualizing a complex architectural space or a detailed mechanical system. I am particularly drawn to the challenge of using 3D Max to create immersive architectural visualizations. For example, in a recent project, I used 3D Max to model and render a multi-story building, focusing on realistic lighting and materials to enhance the visual impact. I thrive on complexity, viewing each obstacle as an opportunity for growth. During my Revit project to design a small town, I overcame the challenge of coordinating multiple building models by implementing a structured BIM workflow, which improved collaboration and reduced errors. As a recent graduate, I am eager to begin my career with a strong commitment to hard work and rapid professional development, aiming to quickly become a valuable asset and advance to leadership roles.
